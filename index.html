I am a third-year PhD student in Economics at the Düsseldorf Institute for Competition Economics, supervised by professor Joel Stiebale. My research interests are in the fields of empirical industrial organization, corporate finance, international trade and innovation economics.

I completed my bachelor’s degree in International Business Studies at the University of Paderborn (2016) and my master’s degree in Economics at the Heinrich-Heine University in Düsseldorf (2019).
